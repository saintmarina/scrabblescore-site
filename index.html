Scrabble Score Calculator

Your simple and accurate way to track Scrabble games

About

Scrabble Score Calculator helps players easily track scores while playing Scrabble or other word games. It replaces traditional pen-and-paper score sheets with a modern, intuitive experience on both iOS and Android.
